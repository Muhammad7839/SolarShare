About SolarShare

We build the allocation layer between households, developers, and utility credit infrastructure.

Clean generation is scaling. Access remains inefficient. SolarShare focuses on the financial coordination layer that routes existing community-solar value to more households with less friction.

Operating Thesis

The limiting factor in many CDG markets is no longer generation buildout, but enrollment quality, allocation velocity, and coordination across fragmented processes.

SolarShare is designed to standardize those workflows without requiring households to become energy experts.

What we optimize

  • Faster matching between household demand and available credit supply
  • Transparent recommendation logic for trust and conversion
  • Operational clarity for developers and advisors
  • State-by-state expansion with reusable workflow patterns

Why this model scales

  • Asset-light model built on existing market infrastructure
  • Lower marginal cost per additional subscriber flow
  • Defensibility through integration and process standardization
  • Multi-territory replication from a shared platform core

Execution Roadmap

Phase 1: Territory launch

Ship a utility-compatible workflow, secure first developer partner, validate initial subscriber base.

Phase 2: Standardize operations

Expand to additional CDG territories and harden adapter + ops tooling for repeatability.

Phase 3: National replication

Scale as a shared clean-energy allocation infrastructure layer across multiple states.
